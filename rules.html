Punishments

Rule violations are handled progressively based on the severity and frequency of offenses:

  • First offense: Warning in chat or DM
  • Second offense: 1-3 day temporary ban
  • Third offense: 7 day temporary ban
  • Severe offenses: Permanent ban (appeals available after 30 days)

The admin team has the final say in all moderation decisions. If you believe a punishment was unjust, you can appeal through our Discord server.
