How Your Donation Helps

When you donate to Sandwi4 SMP, you directly support the infrastructure that keeps our server running. Your contribution helps us:

  • Maintain reliable server hardware and hosting
  • Fund security and anti-cheat systems
  • Support staff who moderate and manage the community
  • Develop new features and improvements
  • Create and run exciting events for the community
  • Keep backups and ensure data safety

Important: Donations do not provide gameplay advantages. All privileges are cosmetic or convenience-based only.

Payment Methods

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • PayPal
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Google Pay
  • Apple Pay

All transactions are secure and processed by trusted payment providers. You'll receive your in-game rewards immediately after payment confirmation.
