About Sandwi4 SMP

Discover what makes our server special

Our Mission

Sandwi4 SMP was founded with a simple goal: to create a welcoming, creative, and secure Minecraft survival multiplayer experience. We believe that the best gaming communities are built on respect, fair play, and shared passion for the game.

Our server provides a vanilla+ experience that maintains the pure essence of Minecraft while offering quality-of-life improvements that make the experience even better. We focus on creating an environment where players can collaborate, build amazing structures, and forge lasting friendships.

Server Features

  • Vanilla+ gameplay with carefully selected plugins
  • Anti-grief protection and security measures
  • Regular events and competitions
  • Active and responsive admin team
  • Discord integration for better community connection
  • Custom skins and cosmetics support
  • Voice chat capability through integrated systems
  • Fair donation system that doesn't affect gameplay
  • Automatic backups to prevent data loss
  • Seasonal updates and new content

Our History

Sandwi4 SMP launched in early 2024 with a small group of dedicated players. What started as a tight-knit community quickly grew into one of the most beloved Minecraft SMP servers in the region. Our success is built on transparency, fair moderation, and continuous improvement based on player feedback.

Over the past years, we've hosted numerous successful seasons, memorable events, and watched our community grow from dozens to hundreds of active players. Season 2 marks an exciting new chapter with even more features, better infrastructure, and expanded opportunities for all players to contribute to the server's legacy.
